LIFE EXPECTANCY

Life expectancy is the number of years an individual can
expect to live.

The data in this chosen dataset was collected from the
Global Health Observatory (GHO) data repository under
the World Health Organization (WHO) and from United
Nation website.

This dataset contains data from year 2000 to 2015 for 193
countries for further analysis.

All the predicting variables in this dataset had been divided
into several broad categories, such as Immunization related factors, mortality factors, economical factors, and also social factors.
